Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about Signova

How secure are my documents?

Your documents are protected with bank-level 256-bit encryption, SOC 2 compliance, and comprehensive audit trails. We maintain the highest security standards in the industry.

Are electronic signatures legally binding?

Yes, electronic signatures created with Signova are legally binding and compliant with e-signature laws worldwide, including ESIGN Act, eIDAS, and other international regulations.

Can I integrate Signova with my existing tools?

Absolutely! Signova integrates with 1000+ popular business tools including Salesforce, HubSpot, Google Drive, Dropbox, and more. We also provide robust API access for custom integrations.

What happens if I exceed my document limit?

You can easily upgrade your plan at any time. We'll also notify you before you reach your limit and provide options to purchase additional documents or upgrade to a higher tier.

Do you offer customer support?

Yes! We provide email support for all plans, priority support for Professional plans, and 24/7 phone support for Enterprise customers. Our team is here to help you succeed.
