Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about Signova AI.

How does the AI field detection work?

Our proprietary AI technology uses machine learning algorithms to analyze document structure, content, and context to automatically identify form fields. The system recognizes patterns in documents to determine where fields should be placed, what type of fields they should be, and how they should be named.

Is Signova AI secure and compliant?

Yes, Signova AI is built with security and compliance at its core. We use industry-standard encryption for all data in transit and at rest. Our platform is compliant with major regulations including HIPAA, GDPR, SOC2, and more, making it suitable for use in regulated industries.

Can Signova AI integrate with our existing systems?

Absolutely. Signova AI offers comprehensive API access and pre-built connectors for popular business systems including Salesforce, Microsoft 365, Google Workspace, and many others. Our team can also build custom integrations for your specific needs.

How much time can we save with Signova AI?

Most organizations see a 75% reduction in document preparation time when using Signova AI compared to traditional document signing solutions. This translates to significant cost savings and improved efficiency for your team.

Do you offer white-labeling for enterprise clients?

Yes, our Enterprise plan includes white-labeling options that allow you to customize the platform with your brand colors, logo, and domain. This creates a seamless experience for your clients and employees.

How quickly can we get started with Signova AI?

Most clients are up and running within 24-48 hours of signing up. Our onboarding team will guide you through the setup process, and the intuitive interface makes it easy for your team to start using the platform immediately.
